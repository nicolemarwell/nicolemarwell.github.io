Mismeasuring Impact
Latest book · 2025

Mismeasuring Impact

Nicole P. Marwell & Jennifer E. Mosley · Stanford University Press

Nonprofits are increasingly pressed to prove their worth through randomized controlled trials. This book explains why the “gold standard” of evaluation is so often the wrong tool for the nonprofit sector — what happens inside organizations that take part, the equity problems it creates, and what to do instead.

About

Organizations, and the making of the city

“Cities can be understood as complex and shifting sets of interorganizational relations.”

Marwell studies how organizations mediate the core processes of urban life — economic production, public-goods distribution, community formation, and democratic representation — drawing on sociology, organization studies, political science, public administration, and technology studies.
