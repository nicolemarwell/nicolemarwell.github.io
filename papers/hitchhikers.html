The Hitchhiker’s Guide to Analyzing the FCC Broadband Data Collection

Jonatas Marques, Alexis Schrubbe, Nicole P. Marwell, & Nick Feamster

Proceedings of the 52nd Research Conference on Communications, Information and Internet Policy (TPRC)

Conference proceedings
SSRN ↗

Abstract

A practical guide to the structure, limitations, and analytical possibilities of the FCC Broadband Data Collection — the most comprehensive federal effort to map U.S. internet availability — giving researchers and policymakers the tools to use it responsibly.
